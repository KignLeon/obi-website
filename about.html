About Old School Boxing

At Old School Boxing Gym, we believe boxing is more than just a sport — it’s a discipline, a lifestyle, and a proven path to strength, confidence, and resilience. Since opening our doors in 2008, we’ve helped athletes, beginners, and everyday people push past their limits and discover just how far they can go.

We don’t just teach boxing. We fuse boxing fundamentals with high-energy fitness training, creating an innovative, results-driven experience that leaves you stronger, sharper, and more motivated every single session.

Our Mission

To empower every member who walks through our doors — from first-timers to seasoned fighters — by providing world-class boxing instruction in a fun, supportive, and high-energy environment. We’re here to help you:

  • Learn the art and science of boxing, step by step.
  • Build real strength, speed, and endurance.
  • Transform your mindset as much as your body.
  • Leave every session feeling unstoppable.

Our Vision

To be San Diego’s home for boxing and fitness — a place where hard work meets community, and where every member achieves more than they thought possible. Our vision is bigger than just training:

  • Create a culture where respect, grit, and growth thrive.
  • Welcome beginners while forging champions through discipline.
  • Foster a community where members push and support one another.
  • Ensure everyone leaves sharper, stronger, and more confident than when they walked in.
